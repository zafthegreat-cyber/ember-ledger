## Intake Notes

1. Record the page or feature where the tester noticed the issue.
2. Use `blocker` only when the tester cannot sign in, cannot access an approved area, loses data, sees private data they should not see, or hits a blank/crashed screen.
3. Do not paste secrets, env values, private family details, full receipts, private addresses, or admin-only data.
4. Add the fix commit after a fix lands, then move the row to `Recently Fixed`.
